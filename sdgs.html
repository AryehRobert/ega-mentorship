🌟 EGA Mentorship Int'l

🌱 EGA Mentorship is a global leadership development program designed to empower young individuals to become effective leaders and entrepreneurs with cross-cultural understanding to drive positive change in achieving the SDGs in all communities worldwide.

🌍 We are passionate about building a network of changemakers who are connected to be equipped to tackle the world’s most pressing challenges.

💡 EGA Mentorship seeks to ignite leadership potential, fostering a global network of changemakers tackling pressing issues.

📚 The program empowers aspiring leaders in entrepreneurship and basic tech skills through problem-solving activities targeting societal, national, and global issues.

📘 Program Overview

🎓 Our mentorship offers a holistic leadership and entrepreneurship development journey.

🙋 Eligibility - Who is eligible?

✨ Open to individuals from all walks of life who are passionate about impact. Leadership is universal!

🌟 01. Aspiring Leaders

🚀 02. Current Leaders

🌱 03. Changemakers

💼 04. Entrepreneurs

🚀 Our Mission

  • 🌟 Develop visionary youth leaders with practical skills and global mindsets.
  • 💪 Empower underrepresented youth to lead SDG initiatives.
  • 📣 Amplify youth voices in global forums and decision-making spaces.
  • 🌍 Nurture professionals committed to sustainability and innovation.
  • ❤️ Instill values of integrity, compassion, and ethical leadership.
  • 🧭 Equip agents of change with tools to accelerate progress on SDGs in Africa.
  • 🌐 Build a thriving global community of future-forward leaders.
  • 🤝 Create real-world solutions through collaboration with global partners.
